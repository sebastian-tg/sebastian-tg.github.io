About me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Zentrum für Astronomie at Heidelberg University. I am part of the MUSTANG research group where I investigate how galaxies shape the properties of their star clusters populations, and how globular clusters can be used to learn about the assembly of galaxies and their dark matter haloes.

Research Interests

  • Reconstructing the assembly of galaxies using the dynamics of their stars and GCs
  • Using HI kinematics of galaxies in volume-limited surveys to understand dark matter and structure formation
  • The role of simulations in the interpretation of observations
